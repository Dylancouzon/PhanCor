Our Company

PHANCOR MEDIA is a collective of marketing professionals, designers, software developers, and event planners that have come together to create a strategic, turn-key and full-service marketing solution for corporate companies and local businesses.

Even though each industry is so uniquely different, the company/consumer relationship will always be the same. It is based on acquiring and retaining top-of-mind presence, stimulating and empowering conversation and engagement between the company and customer and ultimately create a lasting impression. We do this by first assessing and researching each of our clients unique needs and then apply our proprietary discover and planning process and create an innovative turn-key solution that meets our clients goals and puts them one step ahead of their competitors.
